    How to Use the Wesnoth Animation Manager +

    This tool allows you to create professional battle animations for The Battle for Wesnoth. Upload your images, special effects, and sounds, then arrange them on the timeline. Preview your animation and export it in your preferred format.

    1. Upload your unit sprite images (PNG format with transparency)
    2. Add special effects and sound files
    3. Arrange your frames on the timeline
    4. Preview the animation with playback controls
    5. Export as GIF, WML, or sprite sheet
